SDG 4: Quality Education

StudyManager is designed to help students manage their academic responsibilities more effectively, providing a structured way to organize tasks, track deadlines, and plan study sessions, which will improve overall academic performance. Aligning to SDG 4’s goal of ensuring that learners receive the support and tools they need to succeed in their education, promoting a more organized, accessible, and empowering learning experience.

Personalized Boards

Create a personalized board in accordance to the user’s needs. Get the ability to keep multiple subjects or work related tasks organized in a neat manner.

To-Do List

Manage and organize tasks using a to-do list. Add cards of choice and insert descriptions of your tasks. Keep track of deadlines and have the ability to drag and drop tasks into the appropriate table.

Task Reminders

Never miss an important work. Displays incomplete tasks 3 days before its deadline. View what board and which tasks are incomplete along with the date of which is due.

Progress Bar

View the progress you made in a specific board. See how many tasks you’ve completed and how many more you need to complete.
